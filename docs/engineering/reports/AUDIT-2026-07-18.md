Some are intentionally anonymous (analytics ingest, log-error, contact, health, img). The **8 `hooks/agents/*` cron endpoints** and the two `ai/*-tick` cron endpoints should have `cron-auth` middleware — the middleware file `src/middleware/cron-auth.ts` exists but a grep confirms it is not referenced inside these handlers. Needs file-by-file confirmation before fixing.

### P1-2 — 211 Supabase linter warnings
`supabase--linter` reports **211 issues**, dominated by `0028_anon_security_definer_function_executable` — public `EXECUTE` on `SECURITY DEFINER` functions callable by `anon`. There are **220 `SECURITY DEFINER` functions** in `public` (verified via `pg_proc`). SEC-P1-003 batch reports claim EXECUTE was revoked on 57 of them; the linter suggests coverage is incomplete. Requires per-function classification before revoking — not this turn.

Also present:
- `WARN 1: Extension in Public` (1 occurrence — likely `pg_trgm` or `vector`; benign but flagged).
- `WARN 2: RLS Policy Always True` — at least `trust_pages / product_gallery_images / medical_entities` INSERT/UPDATE paths have `qual='true'` or `qual IS NULL` per `pg_policies`. Some SELECT `USING(true)` are intentional public reads and were excluded by the linter itself.
